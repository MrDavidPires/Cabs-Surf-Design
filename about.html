Our Club

Portuguese Family Centre is a community club in Brisbane established in 1978. The club was created to support the Portuguese community in Australia and to share our Portuguese gastronomy, hospitality, culture and traditional events. Join us for a genuine Portuguese experience. Everyone is welcome!
Our Events

Every weekend you can taste the most traditional Portuguese dishes among cod fish, sardines and Portuguese chicken.

We will regularly announce special events, such as live Portuguese bands, dance and the most traditional Portuguese celebrations: Popular Saints Festival, Carnival, Pine Cone Ball, among many others.

You can also celebrate with us all your favourite calendar events: New Year’s Eve, Easter, Mother’s days and Christmas.
